Hello people!

Think of the app as you Shopping App, you want to shop or see your previous orders. For that, the app requires you to login.

When the application loaded, I deliberately didn't call the Auth Server to check for session.

Once you click on Login Button, it will redirect you to Authorization Server, The call should look like:

/oauth2/auth?client_id=app_client&redirect_uri=http://localhost:8082/auth/handleOauth&state=foo&scope=openid&nonce=bar&response_type=token
